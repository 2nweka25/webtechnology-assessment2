GOA Tourism Boom

Eco-Tourism

Eco-tourism is a nature-based form of specialty travel, Eco-tourism is not only nature-based it should also have a minimal impact on the environment at worst and at best can be a benefit to local communities and aid restoration and protection of the ecosystem which this form of tourism benefits off.


Ecotourism is about uniting conservation, communities, and sustainable travel. This means that those who implement, participate in and market ecotourism activities should adopt the following ecotourism principles: Minimize physical, social, behavioral, and psychological impacts. Build environmental and cultural awareness and respect. Provide positive experiences for both visitors and hosts. Provide direct financial benefits for conservation. Generate financial benefits for both local people and private industry. Deliver memorable interpretative experiences to visitors that help raise sensitivity to host countriesâ€™ political, environmental, and social climates. Design, construct and operate low-impact facilities. Recognize the rights and spiritual beliefs of the Indigenous People in your community and work in partnership with them to create empowerment.

Village Tourism

villages act as the roots of a country. villages are the mirror of country's culture , art , foods and natural beauty. In ancient India, the villages determined the economic conditions of kingdoms but the invasion towns and cities started growing at the cost of villages. Still the real soul of our country lives in villages. therefore in the village tourism we get to know the real culture and rich heritage of the nation.
